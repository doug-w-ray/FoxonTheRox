Libations Enthusiast & Trainer

Hello, I'm' Roxanne Read, AKA The Fox on the Rox. An international mixologist with over 14 years experience working as a bartender, mixologist, bar manager, and trainer. We all know you’re busy opening and running venues. Barely any time for training bartenders or creating world-class cocktails. So let me take care of that, it's kind of my thing!

Up to 365 days/year

I am available for a conversation or consultation any day of the week. We will assess your needs over the phone and determine the best course of action moving forward.

In-person training

I am able to provide both in-person training, as well as simply providing a training manual based on your venues theme and drinks list. Training manuals will be provided for all in-person training requests.

My Libations

Designing and implementing world-class cocktails is my passion. Let me come in and create and integrate specialized cocktails that will have your customers begging for more!

Acting, anyone?

I have an acting degree that goes with my years of experience. I am able to be a part of any marketing endeavours you may have and understand how to showcase your venue in the best way possible.
